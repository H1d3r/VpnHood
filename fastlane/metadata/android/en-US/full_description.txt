<p><i>VpnHood</i> is an open-source VPN on GitHub that focus on circumventing severe internet censorship. Our goal is to create a VPN that no Firewall can detect.</p><ul><li>Easy Installation; Just install and press connect</li><li>Undetectable on private IP</li><li>Split Tunneling by Apps &amp; Countries</li><li>No ADS</li></ul><p><i>VpnHood</i> offers a few free public servers that change the IPs from time to time.
